How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two 1s, 2s, 3s, 4s, 5s, 6s or 7s touch, they merge into one, keeping their value! Divide tiles merge with all tiles to make more divide tiles!

Note: The game on this site is the real version of 12's first game.
